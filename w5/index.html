A Message from Night City

The Legend of David Martinez

David Martinez was just a kid from Santo Domingo trying to survive Night City. After losing his mother, he found a family in Maine's edgerunner crew and fell in love with Lucy. He pushed his body beyond its limits with the Sandevistan military cyberware, slowly losing himself to cyberpsychosis but never losing his heart. His final stand at Arasaka Tower became the stuff of legend. Night City never forgot him. Neither did we.
